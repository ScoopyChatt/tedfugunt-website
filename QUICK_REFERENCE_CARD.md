## KEY PHRASES TO USE

- "I reviewed your website and spotted..."
- "...your competition is probably already doing this"
- "...most of my clients see 30-40% more leads in the first month"
- "...zero obligation, just a quick look"
- "...I'd rather show you than try to explain it"
- "...does that make sense?"
- "...fair?"

---

## KEY PHRASES TO AVOID

- "AI" (unless they ask about it specifically)
- "Chatbot" (say "system" instead)
- "Artificial intelligence" (too technical)
- "Guaranteed" (you can't guarantee leads)
- "Limited time offer" (feels salesy)
- "Sign up today" (too pushy)
- "We're different from other agencies" (they don't care)

## AFTER THEY SAY YES

1. Send calendar invite with demo link
2. Send email confirming: "Looking forward to showing you what we built. Here's the link to preview before our call if you want to get a head start."
3. Before call: Review their business, look at their reviews, prepare demo-specific talking points
4. During demo: Ask discovery questions, show real scenarios, focus on outcomes not features
5. After demo: "What questions do you have?" Wait. Then: "Does this feel like something worth moving forward with?"

## YOUR CLOSE RATE TARGET

- Calls that connect: ~20-30%
- Demos booked: ~30-40% of those who connect
- Demos that convert to clients: ~50-70%

**Example:** 50 calls → 12 conversations → 4 demos → 2-3 clients

If you're not hitting these, adjust:
- Are you talking to the right person?
- Are you being specific enough about the opportunity?
- Is your demo actually compelling?
- Are you asking for the close?
