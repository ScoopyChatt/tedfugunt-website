## YOUR COMPLETE WORKFLOW

### STEP 1: PREPARE (Before You Call)

**Time: 10 minutes per prospect**

1. Open Ted Fugunt's current website (tedfugunt.com)
2. Spend 3 minutes looking at it. Identify 2-3 specific weaknesses:
   - "Your site doesn't emphasize emergency service"
   - "There's no way for someone to reach you after hours"
   - "Your design looks outdated compared to competitors"
3. Open **QUICK_REFERENCE_CARD.md** on your screen
4. Prepare your opening line using the template provided
5. Have the website mockup (WEBSITE_MOCKUP.html) open in another tab, ready to share

**Checklist:**
- ✓ Know their business model (emergency + planned maintenance)
- ✓ Know their service areas (Chattanooga, Hixson, Signal Mountain, etc.)
- ✓ Have specific critiques of their current site
- ✓ Have your opening ready
- ✓ Have the demo link ready to send
- ✓ Have your calendar link ready

---

### STEP 2: CALL (5-10 minutes)

**Use QUICK_REFERENCE_CARD.md for exact wording**

1. **Opening (30 seconds):** "Hi [Name], Brandon with Sol Invictus AI. I reviewed your website and spotted a major opportunity..."

2. **The Hook (1 minute):** Mention the specific problem: "You're losing emergency leads because people can't reach you after hours."

3. **Objection or Interest (1-3 minutes):** Use the objection playbook if they push back. Most will say "tell me more" or ask questions.

4. **The Close (30 seconds):** "The demo takes 15 minutes. You'll see exactly what this looks like for Ted Fugunt. Does Tuesday or Wednesday work better?"

5. **If they say yes:** Get their email, send calendar invite with demo link

6. **If they say maybe:** "Let me just send you the link. Take a look and let me know."

**Key phrases:**
- "I reviewed your website and spotted..."
- "You're leaving money on the table with..."
- "Most of my clients see 30-40% more leads in the first month"
- "Zero obligation, just a quick look"

---

### STEP 3: DEMO CALL (15-20 minutes)

**Use TED_FUGUNT_COLD_OUTREACH_COMPLETE.md → "Demo Walkthrough Outline" section**

**Before the call:**
- Test that WEBSITE_MOCKUP.html loads on your screen
- Have your discovery questions ready (printed or on screen)
- Know the key talking points for each section

**During the demo:**
1. **Opening (1 min):** "What I'm about to show you is a complete rebuild..."
2. **Homepage walkthrough (2 min):** Show modern design, emergency CTA, service areas
3. **Live chat demo (5-7 min):** Walk through real-world scenarios
4. **Lead dashboard (2 min):** Show organization and efficiency gains
5. **Results/Metrics (2 min):** Share numbers from similar companies
6. **Ask discovery questions (3 min):** "How many leads are you getting now?" etc.
7. **Close (1 min):** "Does this feel like something worth exploring?"

**What to emphasize:**
- NOT "AI" or technology
- INSTEAD: "More booked calls," "Emergency leads after hours," "Pre-qualified leads"
- Show real scenarios (10pm AC emergency landing on site)
- Let them see the chat widget actually work
- Tie it to their business model (emergency revenue is highest margin)

---

### STEP 4: FOLLOW-UP (Depends on their response)

**If they said YES:**
- Send them a formal proposal within 24 hours
- Schedule a "next steps" call for 2-3 days out
- Start building their site (they'll be excited to see progress)

**If they said "I like it, thinking about it":**
- Wait 3 days
- Send a text: "Hey [Name], still thinking about that demo?"
- Reference a specific moment from the call: "Remember that scenario where someone calls at 10pm and we capture the lead?"
- Move to close: "What's the hesitation? Is it investment, timeline, or something else?"

**If they said NO:**
- Don't give up yet
- Send: "Hey [Name], I know you said no, but send over your email and I'll keep you in the loop on similar companies. You might change your mind."
- Add to nurture list
- Check back in 6 months

## OBJECTION QUICK-REFERENCE

**"We already have a website"**
→ "I know. It's not capturing after-hours calls."

**"We don't need AI"**
→ "You don't need AI. You need fewer missed calls and better-qualified leads."

**"Send me information"**
→ "Better as a live demo. 15 minutes next week?"

**"How much does it cost?"**
→ "Redirect what you're already spending on leads. Most see 3-4x ROI first quarter."

**"We're not interested"**
→ "I get it. Let me just send you the demo link. Might change your mind."

**"How do I know it works?"**
→ "You'll know best once we prove it for 90 days."

## DAILY CALLING SCHEDULE

### Monday-Friday (Pick 2-3 hours for calling)

**Best times:** 9-11am or 2-4pm (they're less busy)

**Avoid:** 12-1pm (lunch), 4pm+ (closing up), before 8am

**Daily goal:** 15-20 calls → 3-5 conversations → 1-2 demos booked

## SUCCESS METRICS

**What good looks like:**

- **Call connect rate:** 20-30% of calls result in a conversation
- **Demo booking rate:** 30-40% of conversations book a demo
- **Close rate:** 50-70% of demos close
- **Example:** 100 calls → 25 conversations → 8 demos → 4-5 clients

**If you're not hitting these:**
- Are you calling the right person? (Owner/manager, not office staff)
- Are you specific enough about the problem?
- Is your demo compelling? (Test it)
- Are you asking for the close?
- Are you following up enough?

## RED FLAGS (Don't Waste Time Here)

- They're not the decision-maker AND won't intro you to the owner
- They've said no twice with no reconsideration
- They keep asking for proposals without seeing the demo
- They're shopping on price, not value
- They're dismissive about their current website being a problem

→ **Move on.** Plenty of other HVAC companies want to grow.
