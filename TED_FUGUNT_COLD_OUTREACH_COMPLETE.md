## 10. DISCOVERY QUESTIONS FOR THE DEMO CALL

Ask these *during* the initial call or early in the demo to understand their business and position the solution correctly.

### Current Lead Flow
- How many website leads do you currently get per week/month?
- What percentage of people who visit your site actually contact you?
- Do you track where your best leads come from (phone, form, text)?

### After-Hours Business
- Do you get calls/requests for emergency service outside of 8am-4pm?
- What happens to those calls right now? (Do they go to voicemail? Does someone answer?)
- How many emergency calls do you estimate you miss or can't respond to immediately?

### Service Mix & Revenue
- What percentage of your business is emergency repair vs. planned maintenance/replacements?
- Which is more profitable — emergency service or planned replacements?
- Are you trying to grow emergency calls, maintenance plans, or replacements? Or all three?

### Lead Qualification
- When someone contacts you, how much time does your team spend figuring out what they actually need?
- What questions do you ask most often? (What type of service? What area? Do you offer financing? Etc.)
- How many leads do you get that aren't qualified or aren't your ideal customer?

### Current Systems
- What CRM or scheduling system do you use?
- How do you currently manage inbound requests?
- Is someone manually handling all inquiries, or do you have any automation?

### Growth & Goals
- What's your average job size for a replacement vs. emergency repair?
- How many more service calls per week would meaningfully impact your business? (5? 10? 20?)
- What's your biggest bottleneck right now — getting leads, converting them, or servicing them?

### Competitive Position
- How do you compete against bigger HVAC companies in your area?
- What's your main advantage over competitors?
- Do you think customers perceive you as tech-forward or traditional?
